# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Love Over Exile ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit loveoverexile.com.

## Who We Are

Love Over Exile is a website and book platform run by Malcolm Smith, focused on parental alienation awareness, support, and resources for alienated parents.

Website: [loveoverexile.com](https://loveoverexile.com)
Contact: Use the contact form on this website.

## What Information We Collect

### Information You Give Us

- **Email address** — when you sign up for the book waitlist or newsletter
- **Name** — if you provide it when signing up or contacting us
- **Messages** — when you use the contact form

### Information Collected Automatically

When you visit this website, standard web server logs may collect:

- Your IP address
- Browser type and version
- Pages you visit and time spent
- Referring website

This is standard for all websites and is used only to understand how the site is being used.

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information only for:

- Sending you updates about the book launch (if you signed up for the waitlist)
- Sending the newsletter (if you subscribed)
- Responding to your messages
- Improving the website

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

## Email and Newsletter

If you sign up for the waitlist or newsletter, you can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any email. We use [Kit (ConvertKit)] to manage our email list, which is subject to their own privacy policy.

## Cookies

This website may use basic cookies to:

- Remember your preferences
- Understand how visitors use the site (analytics)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

## Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union, you have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, use the contact form on this website.

## Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information. However, no transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page shows when it was last updated. Continued use of the website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Contact

For any privacy questions or requests, please use the contact form at [loveoverexile.com/contact](/contact).
