---

## The Visitor's Journey

The site is designed around three types of visitor:

| Visitor | Where they are | What they need first |
|---------|---------------|----------------------|
| **The Acute Crisis Parent** | Just been alienated / charges just filed | Validation, understanding, "is this what's happening to me?" |
| **The Survivor** | In the middle of it, months or years in | Practical tools, tactics, survival strategies |
| **The Seeker** | Looking for deeper meaning and healing | Inner transformation, acceptance, forgiveness |

All three paths lead to the book. The site is built around guiding each visitor through their journey — and converting them to the waitlist.
